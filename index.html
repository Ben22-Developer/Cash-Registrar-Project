CASH REGISTER WEB APP

Suppose you are at a bookstore buying a novel for $15 and you've $20. The cashier enters your price into the cash register.
The cash register calculates that you get $5 in change and triggers the cash drawer to open, so that you get your exchange. With this online application, the cash register automatically calculates the customer's payment and ensures change is given based on the available bills and coins in the cash drawer.
For example, if $545 needs to be returned, and the drawer contains 6 bills of $100, 5 bills of $50, and 5 coins of $5, the cash register combines these denominations to pay back the exact amount, and the system updates the cash drawer in the user interface (UI) to reflect the remaining cash.
If there isn’t enough money in the cash drawer, the transaction won’t process, and no change will be given.
Similarly, if the customer's payment matches the exact price, no change is required, and the UI remains unchanged.
This functionality enhances payment efficiency, ensures accuracy, and dynamically updates the system to maintain transparency.
